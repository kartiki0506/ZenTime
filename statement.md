# Problem Statement
In this Modern Era, Students and various working individuals often struggle to manage various tasks.  With loads of tasks it become a tough nut to schedule and remember every task with its deadline. This is due to inefficient time management and planning. Due to overlapping commitments, irregular schedules, and lack of personalized planning individuals struggle to meet their ability 
Existing solutions either focus on rigid templates or overlook individual preferences and wellness activities, causing stress and reducing productivity.

The Zenith application provides an interactive interface to the user, built using Pythonâ€™s Tkinter library.
Its scope includes:
* Allowing users to create, edit, and visualize personalized timetables for each day.
* Automatically arranging classes and tasks based on urgency, due dates and priority
* Tracking completion of tasks and providing day analytics for productivity assessment.
* Providing an interactive digital To-Do List application to help user stay organized and stress free.
# Target Users
* Students: To plan their day to day time (study sessions, lectures, and personal activities).
* Working Professionals: To manage meetings, deadlines, and personal commitments.
* Teachers/Instructors: To organize teaching schedules and tasks efficiently.
* General Users: Anyone who needs structured daily planning with automated scheduling.
# High-Level Features
* Interactive GUI: Sidebar to navigate between Dashboard, Editor, and Analytics Tabs.
* Task Management: Add, remove, and mark tasks as completed.
* Analytics Dashboard: Visualize daily time distribution via pie charts.
* Customization: Allows users to input classes, meals, and flexible time entries.
* Real-time Feedback: Provides an update message upon saving schedules.
